Overview

RationFit sells nutrition recipes on the US market. They were leaving money on the table after every checkout. I built a custom Shopify app from scratch that presents targeted post-purchase offers the moment a customer completes their order. Result: average order value increased by 35% — completely on autopilot.

What I Did

Post-Purchase Upsell Engine

Built a smart offer engine that triggers immediately after checkout with personalised discount offers — catching customers at peak buying intent when they are most likely to add.

Custom Admin Panel

Engineered a clean admin panel so the RationFit team can independently manage products, names, descriptions and prices in the upsell flow. Zero developer dependency after handoff.

React & Shopify Integration

Built entirely with React and JavaScript, integrated directly with Shopify API. No third-party upsell tools, no monthly fees — complete ownership for the client.

4 Days to Production

Designed, built and delivered a production-ready custom app in 4 days. Full UI matching the store brand, native Shopify checkout feel, ready to ship.
