Overview

CASTO is a premium men's aesthetics brand targeting the US market. The brief was simple — no generic templates, no compromises. Build a unique dark masculine identity from zero that resonates with the audience and drives sales. The result is a fully custom storefront that looks nothing like any other Shopify store.

What I Did

Custom Shopify Theme

Built a complete theme from scratch using Liquid, JavaScript and CSS — every line written by hand. Zero template dependencies, zero compromises on the design vision.

Dark Premium UI/UX

Designed a high-end dark interface with sharp serif typography and a scientific authority aesthetic. Every design decision made to match the brand's brutal masculine positioning.

Custom Interactive Elements

Engineered a dynamic marquee banner, unique product cards with smooth interactions, and a seamless Add to Bag flow that removes every friction point from the purchase journey.

US Market Optimisation

Fully optimised for the competitive US e-commerce market — lightning-fast load times, perfect mobile experience on every device, ready to compete at the highest level.

Result

A storefront that stands out in a sea of identical Shopify stores.
