Overview

OasisGlow is a seasonal e-commerce store built for the US market selling home, beach and pool lifestyle products. The challenge was to build fast, launch fast, and generate revenue before the season ends. Result: $6,000 per month in profit across a 3-month campaign, fully automated through dropshipping via DSers.

What I Did

Custom Shopify Theme

Designed and built a complete custom theme from scratch — clean, lifestyle-focused visual identity built to convert summer shoppers on both desktop and mobile.

DSers Dropshipping Pipeline

Configured full DSers dropshipping setup for seamless automated order fulfilment. Zero manual work — orders processed and fulfilled automatically around the clock.

Klaviyo & Email Marketing

Integrated Klaviyo for lead capture, pop-up forms and automated email flows that recovered abandoned carts and drove repeat purchases throughout the season.

Upsell & AOV Optimisation

Implemented upsell apps to maximise average order value on every transaction. Every customer touchpoint was optimised to spend more per visit.

Result

A seasonal machine that ran itself.
